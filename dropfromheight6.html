Calculate Impact

Mathematical and Background Calculations:

1. Time to Impact (t): \( t = \sqrt{\frac{2s}{a}} \)
2. Velocity at Impact (v): \( v = at \)
3. Acceleration at Impact (a): For objects in free fall, acceleration is due to gravity and is constant, typically \(9.81 \, \text{m/s}^2\) on the surface of the Earth.
4. Kinetic Energy (KE): \( KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 \)
5. Impact Force (F): \( F = ma \)
